# Legal launch review

Internal review tracker. This document is not imported, linked, rendered, indexed, or included in the website sitemap.

## Privacy Policy

- Verify the visible field list and required fields against the approved production HubSpot form.
- Confirm the production hosting log configuration and ensure the policy continues to describe it accurately.
- Review the legal-basis wording and any jurisdiction-specific notices that apply across the launch markets.
- Reinspect the approved production form for cookies, browser storage, third-party requests, and consent implications.
- Confirm the complete service-provider list, internal access rules, and disclosures against operating practice.
- Confirm international processing locations, contractual safeguards, and jurisdiction-specific transfer wording against provider contracts.
- Document the operational retention and deletion procedure.
- Confirm applicable privacy rights, exceptions, response processes, and timeframes.

## Terms of Use

- Confirm the chain of title and licences for launch assets, fonts, photographs, illustrations, and contributed materials.
- Review exclusions, limitations, any liability caps, categories of loss, and applicable consumer-law wording.
- Confirm the appropriate court, venue, dispute-resolution process, and treatment of mandatory laws outside California.

## Ongoing change control

- Repeat the legal and technical review if the production host, HubSpot form, analytics, marketing tracking, embedded media, storage behaviour, providers, or service model changes.
- Keep this tracker internal. Do not expose review status through public page content, metadata, structured data, navigation, or design components.
