Reviewed 22 July 2026. These sites are reference points for category expectations, not templates for 42 to reproduce.

## Category patterns worth learning from

1. **Make the problem visible.** Strong pages frame a recognisable operational problem before listing services. Diagnostic graphics, before/after structures and explicit system relationships make abstract consulting easier to understand.
2. **Give services a visual identity.** Bespoke icons and contextual illustrations help visitors scan broad capabilities quickly. The symbol should explain the service, not merely decorate its card.
3. **Show a connected operating system.** The leading sites position HubSpot inside a broader revenue, customer and data system. Their most useful visuals connect teams, data, process and technology.
4. **Use proof close to the claim.** Case studies, metrics and testimonials are treated as structural content rather than a logo strip added at the end. 42 must follow this pattern only when proof is verified and approved.
5. **Use motion to explain relationships.** Progressive disclosure, connector movement, staggered cards and responsive hover states create momentum when they reinforce information hierarchy.
6. **Create one recognisable visual world.** The strongest sites repeat a consistent palette, shape language and image treatment across heroes, icons, cards and diagrams.

## Patterns 42 should avoid

- Generic team or office stock photography
- Unverified reviews, client logos, awards, partner tiers or performance statistics
- Product-interface screenshots presented as if they were client systems
- Loud gradients used as a substitute for composition
- Clip-art icon sets with mixed stroke weights or visual metaphors
- Motion that delays reading, hijacks scrolling or ignores reduced-motion preferences

## 42 response

42 will own a distinct **systems workshop** visual language:

- tactile architectural still-life imagery rather than stock photography;
- warm paper, dark engineered structures, signal-green decisions and restrained blue connections;
- original square-grid line icons derived from data, process, architecture and movement;
- motion based on revealing, connecting, calibrating and resolving;
- explicit content and proof safeguards preserved throughout.

This direction meets the category's expectation for confidence and technical depth while remaining recognisably 42.
